  • Founded in 2004

Demographic Facts

68% of U.S. adults use Facebook

much higher rates than other age groups

  • Founded in 2009
  • Pinterest is a visual discovery engine where users can find and share ideas and inspiration for various interests.

Demographic Facts

35% of U.S. adults use Pinterest

TikTok

  • Founded in 2016
  • TikTok is a short-video sharing app where users can create and share videos, often featuring music, dance, or comedic content.

Demographic Facts
